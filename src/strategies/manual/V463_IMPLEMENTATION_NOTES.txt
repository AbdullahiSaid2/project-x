V463 implementation summary

Core sizing regime
- 5 MNQ on weak / standard setups
- 10 MNQ only on strong setups

10 MNQ qualification
- setup_tier == A
- setup_type in {LONDON_CONTINUATION, NYAM_CONTINUATION, NYPM_CONTINUATION}
- bridge_type in {iFVG / IFVG, C2C3, MSS}
- planned_rr >= 4.0
- stop_points <= 35.0

Everything else stays at 5 MNQ.
