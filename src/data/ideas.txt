# RSI Extremes with Trend Filter
Long when RSI drops below 30 and price is above the 200 EMA, exit when RSI rises back above 50
Short when RSI rises above 70 and price is below the 200 EMA, exit when RSI falls back below 50

# MACD Crossover with Volume
Long when MACD line crosses above the signal line with volume above the 20-bar average confirming the move, exit when MACD crosses back below signal
Short when MACD line crosses below the signal line with volume above the 20-bar average confirming the move, exit when MACD crosses back above signal

# Bollinger Band Squeeze Breakout
Long when Bollinger Band width contracts to a 6-month low squeeze and price breaks and closes above the upper band with volume, stop below the lower band
Short when Bollinger Band width contracts to a 6-month low squeeze and price breaks and closes below the lower band with volume, stop above the upper band

# EMA Cross with RSI Momentum Guard
Long when 9 EMA crosses above 21 EMA and RSI is above 50 confirming upward momentum, exit when 9 EMA crosses back below 21 EMA
Short when 9 EMA crosses below 21 EMA and RSI is below 50 confirming downward momentum, exit when 9 EMA crosses back above 21 EMA

# ICT STRATEGIES
# Inherently directional — each setup is one direction only.
# Long and short versions are separate entries as they should be.
# ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

# Order Blocks
Long at bullish order block: identify the last bearish candle before a strong 3-candle bullish move, price returns to that candle body, enter long with stop below candle low
Short at bearish order block: identify the last bullish candle before a strong 3-candle bearish move, price returns to that candle body, enter short with stop above candle high

# Fair Value Gaps
Long when price retraces into a bullish fair value gap where candle 1 high and candle 3 low do not overlap, enter long at the 50 percent midpoint of the gap
Short when price retraces into a bearish fair value gap where candle 1 low and candle 3 high do not overlap, enter short at the 50 percent midpoint

# Liquidity Sweeps
Long after price sweeps below the most recent swing low then closes back above it within 2 candles, stop below the sweep low
Short after price sweeps above the most recent swing high then closes back below it within 2 candles, stop above the sweep high

# Change of Character
Long on Change of Character: after a series of lower highs and lower lows price breaks and closes above the most recent lower high
Short on Change of Character: after a series of higher highs and higher lows price breaks and closes below the most recent higher low

# Premium and Discount Zone
Long when price is in the lower 50 percent of the 20-bar range and RSI is below 40 and price is above the 50 EMA
Short when price is in the upper 50 percent of the 20-bar range and RSI is above 60 and price is below the 50 EMA

# FVG after Liquidity Sweep
Long when price sweeps a swing low then forms a bullish fair value gap on the next 3 candles and returns to fill the gap
Short when price sweeps a swing high then forms a bearish fair value gap on the next 3 candles and returns to fill the gap

# TECHNICAL INDICATOR STRATEGIES
# All correctly paired. Long only fires in uptrend context, short only in downtrend.
# ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

# StochRSI — trend-filtered so we never long in a downtrend or short in an uptrend
Long when StochRSI crosses above 20 from oversold territory and price is above the 200 EMA, exit when StochRSI crosses above 80
Short when StochRSI crosses below 80 from overbought territory and price is below the 200 EMA, exit when StochRSI crosses below 20

# RSI Divergence — divergence is inherently directional
Long when price makes a lower low but RSI makes a higher low indicating bullish divergence, enter on the next bullish candle close
Short when price makes a higher high but RSI makes a lower high indicating bearish divergence, enter on the next bearish candle close

# MACD Histogram Turning Points
Long when MACD histogram turns from negative to less negative meaning histogram starts rising while still below zero, target the zero line
Short when MACD histogram turns from positive to less positive meaning histogram starts falling while still above zero, target the zero line

# Bollinger Band Squeeze
Long when Bollinger Band width reaches a 6-month low squeeze and price breaks above the upper band with volume
Short when Bollinger Band width reaches a 6-month low squeeze and price breaks below the lower band with volume

# Keltner Channel Breakout
Long when price closes above the upper Keltner Channel which is 20 EMA plus 1.5 ATR with RSI above 50
Short when price closes below the lower Keltner Channel which is 20 EMA minus 1.5 ATR with RSI below 50

# ADX Trend Strength — trades only in direction of the confirmed trend
Long when ADX crosses above 25 indicating a strong trend and price is above the 50 EMA, hold until ADX drops below 20
Short when ADX crosses above 25 indicating a strong trend and price is below the 50 EMA, hold until ADX drops below 20

# CCI Overbought Oversold — symmetric mean reversion
Long when CCI drops below negative 100 and then crosses back above negative 100, targeting the zero line
Short when CCI rises above positive 100 and then crosses back below positive 100, targeting the zero line

# Williams %R — symmetric oscillator
Long when Williams %R drops below negative 80 and crosses back above negative 80, stop below recent swing low
Short when Williams %R rises above negative 20 and crosses back below negative 20, stop above recent swing high

# Parabolic SAR — trend-following trail
Long when Parabolic SAR flips from above price to below price, stop at SAR level, trail stop as SAR moves up
Short when Parabolic SAR flips from below price to above price, stop at SAR level, trail stop as SAR moves down

# Money Flow Index — volume-weighted oscillator
Long when MFI drops below 20 indicating oversold with high volume and then crosses back above 20
Short when MFI rises above 80 indicating overbought with high volume and then crosses back below 80

# Ichimoku Cloud — trend-confirmed entries only
Long when price breaks above the Ichimoku cloud and tenkan-sen is above kijun-sen and chikou span is above price
Short when price breaks below the Ichimoku cloud and tenkan-sen is below kijun-sen and chikou span is below price

# VOLUME AND ORDER FLOW STRATEGIES
# ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

# On-Balance Volume — OBV leads price
Long when OBV makes a new high before price makes a new high, enter long on price confirmation
Short when OBV makes a new low before price makes a new low, enter short on price confirmation

# Volume Spread Analysis — stopping volume is directional
Long when a wide spread down bar closes on its high with very high volume indicating stopping volume
Short when a wide spread up bar closes on its low with very high volume indicating supply entering

# Volume Spike Reversal — spike direction determines trade
Long when volume is 3x the 20-bar average on a down bar and price closes in the upper 25 percent of bar range
Short when volume is 3x the 20-bar average on an up bar and price closes in the lower 25 percent of bar range

# VWAP Reversion — symmetric mean reversion
Long when price drops more than 2 standard deviations below VWAP and volume is declining, target VWAP
Short when price rises more than 2 standard deviations above VWAP and volume is declining, target VWAP

# MEAN REVERSION STRATEGIES
# Symmetric by nature — both directions valid.
# ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

# Z-Score Mean Reversion
Long when the 20-bar z-score of price drops below negative 2.0, exit when z-score returns to zero
Short when the 20-bar z-score of price rises above positive 2.0, exit when z-score returns to zero

# RSI Mean Reversion
Long when RSI drops below 30 and the previous bar RSI was above 30, stop below recent swing low
Short when RSI rises above 70 and the previous bar RSI was below 70, stop above recent swing high

# Distance from Moving Average
Long when price is more than 3 percent below the 20 EMA and RSI is below 40, target the 20 EMA
Short when price is more than 3 percent above the 20 EMA and RSI is above 60, target the 20 EMA

# Bollinger Band Bounce
Long when price touches the lower Bollinger Band with RSI below 35, target middle band
Short when price touches the upper Bollinger Band with RSI above 65, target middle band

# MOMENTUM STRATEGIES
# Momentum works equally in both directions.
# ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

# Dual Momentum — trend-following in both directions
Long when price is above both the 10-bar and 20-bar EMA and both EMAs are rising, hold until both flatten
Short when price is below both the 10-bar and 20-bar EMA and both EMAs are falling, hold until both flatten

# Price Rate of Change
Long when 14-bar rate of change crosses above zero from below and price is above the 50 EMA
Short when 14-bar rate of change crosses below zero from above and price is below the 50 EMA

# Relative Strength vs BTC
Long the asset when its 20-bar return is greater than the 20-bar return of BTC indicating relative outperformance
Short the asset when its 20-bar return is less than the 20-bar return of BTC by more than 5 percent

# CRYPTO-SPECIFIC STRATEGIES
# Funding and OI signals have specific directional logic — handled carefully.
# ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

# Funding Rate Mean Reversion — RSI guards direction
Long when funding rate has been negative for 3 consecutive 8-hour periods meaning shorts are paying longs and RSI is below 40
Short when funding rate has been positive for 3 consecutive 8-hour periods meaning longs are paying shorts and RSI is above 60

# Liquidation Cascade Reversal — direction set by which side was liquidated
Long immediately after long liquidations exceed 10 million USD in 15 minutes meaning sell pressure is exhausted
Short immediately after short liquidations exceed 10 million USD in 15 minutes meaning buy pressure is exhausted

# Open Interest Divergence — OI drop signals exhaustion of the prevailing move
Long when price makes a new low but open interest drops significantly indicating shorts are covering
Short when price makes a new high but open interest drops significantly indicating longs are closing

# High Funding Carry — SHORT ONLY by design, do not add a long version
# Logic: When funding is very high and positive, longs are paying shorts.
# You short the perpetual to collect that payment. A long carry equivalent
# (very negative funding) is already covered by the Funding Rate Mean
# Reversion long entry above. Adding a long here would duplicate that idea.
Short the perpetual when funding rate exceeds 0.1 percent per 8 hours annualised above 50 percent, hold until funding normalises below 0.05 percent per 8 hours

# TREND FOLLOWING STRATEGIES
# ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

# Classic Donchian Breakout
Long when price closes above the 20-period highest high, hold with trailing stop based on the lowest low of the prior 10 bars
Short when price closes below the 20-period lowest low, hold with trailing stop based on the highest high of the prior 10 bars

# Triple EMA Trend Stack
Long when 9 EMA crosses above 21 EMA which is above 55 EMA and all three are pointing up
Short when 9 EMA crosses below 21 EMA which is below 55 EMA and all three are pointing down


# ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
# QUANTITATIVE STRATEGIES
# Symmetric by construction.
# ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

# Volatility Breakout from Open
Long when price moves more than 1.5 ATR above the open within the first 3 bars, stop at the open
Short when price moves more than 1.5 ATR below the open within the first 3 bars, stop at the open

# Mean Reversion after Extreme Move
Long when price drops more than 2 ATR in a single bar and then forms a bullish inside bar signalling exhaustion
Short when price rises more than 2 ATR in a single bar and then forms a bearish inside bar signalling exhaustion

# Regime-Adjusted EMA Cross — only trades in low volatility trending environments
Long when 20 EMA crosses above 50 EMA and ATR is below the 20-bar ATR average indicating low volatility trend
Short when 20 EMA crosses below 50 EMA and ATR is below the 20-bar ATR average indicating low volatility trend
