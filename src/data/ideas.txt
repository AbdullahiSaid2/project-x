# TECHNICAL INDICATOR STRATEGIES
# ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

# StochRSI
Buy when StochRSI crosses above 20 from oversold territory and price is above 200 EMA, sell when StochRSI crosses above 80
Sell when StochRSI crosses below 80 from overbought territory and price is below 200 EMA, cover when StochRSI crosses below 20

# RSI Divergence
Buy when price makes lower low but RSI makes higher low (bullish divergence), enter on the next bullish candle close
Sell when price makes higher high but RSI makes lower high (bearish divergence), enter on the next bearish candle close

# MACD Histogram Turning Points
Buy when MACD histogram turns from negative to less negative (histogram starts rising) while below zero, target zero line
Sell when MACD histogram turns from positive to less positive (histogram starts falling) while above zero

# Bollinger Band Squeeze
Buy when Bollinger Band width reaches 6-month low (squeeze) and price breaks above upper band with volume
Sell when Bollinger Band width reaches 6-month low and price breaks below lower band with volume

# Keltner Channel Breakout
Buy when price closes above the upper Keltner Channel (20 EMA plus 1.5 ATR) with RSI above 50
Sell when price closes below the lower Keltner Channel (20 EMA minus 1.5 ATR) with RSI below 50

# ADX Trend Strength
Buy when ADX crosses above 25 indicating strong trend and price is above 50 EMA, only trade in trend direction
Sell when ADX crosses above 25 and price is below 50 EMA, hold until ADX drops below 20

# CCI Overbought Oversold
Buy when CCI drops below negative 100 and then crosses back above negative 100, targeting zero line
Sell when CCI rises above positive 100 and then crosses back below positive 100, targeting zero line

# Williams %R
Buy when Williams %R drops below negative 80 and crosses back above negative 80, stop below recent low
Sell when Williams %R rises above negative 20 and crosses back below negative 20, stop above recent high

# Parabolic SAR
Buy when Parabolic SAR flips from above price to below price, stop at SAR level, trail stop as SAR moves
Sell when Parabolic SAR flips from below price to above price, stop at SAR level

# Money Flow Index
Buy when MFI drops below 20 indicating oversold with high volume and then crosses back above 20
Sell when MFI rises above 80 indicating overbought with high volume and then crosses back below 80

# Ichimoku Cloud
Buy when price breaks above the Ichimoku cloud, tenkan-sen is above kijun-sen, and chikou span is above price
Sell when price breaks below the Ichimoku cloud, tenkan-sen is below kijun-sen

# VOLUME AND ORDER FLOW STRATEGIES
# ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

# On-Balance Volume
Buy when OBV makes new high before price makes new high (OBV leading price), enter long on price confirmation
Sell when OBV makes new low before price makes new low, enter short on price confirmation

# Volume Spread Analysis
Buy when a wide spread down bar closes on its high with very high volume (stopping volume), price likely to reverse up
Sell when a wide spread up bar closes on its low with very high volume (supply entering), price likely to reverse down

# Volume Spike Reversal
Buy when volume is 3x the 20-bar average on a down bar and price closes in upper 25 percent of bar range
Sell when volume is 3x the 20-bar average on an up bar and price closes in lower 25 percent of bar range

# VWAP Reversion
Buy when price drops more than 2 standard deviations below VWAP and volume is declining, target VWAP
Sell when price rises more than 2 standard deviations above VWAP and volume is declining, target VWAP

# MEAN REVERSION STRATEGIES
# ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

# Z-Score Mean Reversion
Buy when the 20-bar z-score of price drops below negative 2.0, exit when z-score returns to zero
Sell when the 20-bar z-score of price rises above positive 2.0, exit when z-score returns to zero

# RSI Mean Reversion
Buy when RSI drops below 30 and the previous bar RSI was above 30, stop below recent swing low
Sell when RSI rises above 70 and the previous bar RSI was below 70, stop above recent swing high

# Distance from Moving Average
Buy when price is more than 3 percent below the 20 EMA and RSI is below 40, target 20 EMA
Sell when price is more than 3 percent above the 20 EMA and RSI is above 60, target 20 EMA

# Bollinger Band Bounce
Buy when price touches the lower Bollinger Band with RSI below 35, target middle band
Sell when price touches the upper Bollinger Band with RSI above 65, target middle band

# MOMENTUM STRATEGIES
# ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

# Dual Momentum
Buy when price is above both the 10-bar and 20-bar EMA and both EMAs are rising, hold until both flatten
Sell when price is below both the 10-bar and 20-bar EMA and both EMAs are falling

# Price Rate of Change Momentum
Buy when 14-bar rate of change crosses above zero from below, price above 50 EMA
Sell when 14-bar rate of change crosses below zero from above, price below 50 EMA

# Relative Strength Momentum
Buy the asset when its 20-bar return is greater than the 20-bar return of BTC (relative outperformance)
Sell the asset when its 20-bar return is less than the 20-bar return of BTC by more than 5 percent

# CRYPTO-SPECIFIC STRATEGIES
# ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

# Funding Rate Mean Reversion
Buy when funding rate has been negative for 3 consecutive 8-hour periods (shorts paying) and RSI is below 40
Sell when funding rate has been positive for 3 consecutive 8-hour periods (longs paying) and RSI is above 60

# Liquidation Cascade Reversal
Buy immediately after a large liquidation spike where long liquidations exceed 10 million USD in 15 minutes
Sell immediately after a large liquidation spike where short liquidations exceed 10 million USD in 15 minutes

# Open Interest Divergence
Buy when price makes new low but open interest drops significantly (shorts covering), indicates reversal
Sell when price makes new high but open interest drops significantly (longs closing), indicates exhaustion

# High Funding Carry
Short the perpetual when funding rate exceeds 0.1 percent per 8 hours (annualised above 50 percent), hold until funding normalises below 0.05 percent

# TREND FOLLOWING STRATEGIES
# ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

# Classic Trend Following
Buy when price closes above the 20-period highest high (20-bar breakout), hold with trailing stop of 10 bars
Sell short when price closes below the 20-period lowest low, hold with trailing stop of 10 bars

# Triple EMA Trend
Buy when 9 EMA crosses above 21 EMA which is above 55 EMA and all three are pointing up
Sell when 9 EMA crosses below 21 EMA which is below 55 EMA and all three are pointing down

# QUANTITATIVE STRATEGIES
# ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

# Volatility Breakout
Buy when price moves more than 1.5 ATR above the open within the first 3 bars, stop at open
Sell when price moves more than 1.5 ATR below the open within the first 3 bars, stop at open

# Mean Reversion after Extreme Move
Buy when price drops more than 2 ATR in a single bar and then forms a bullish inside bar
Sell when price rises more than 2 ATR in a single bar and then forms a bearish inside bar

# Regime-Adjusted EMA Cross
Buy when 20 EMA crosses above 50 EMA and ATR is below the 20-bar ATR average (low volatility trend)
Sell when 20 EMA crosses below 50 EMA and ATR is below the 20-bar ATR average
